Part I. Introduction

What are FPV drones? FPV Drones are a type of manually controlled drone. Unlike normal drones, you are in complete control of this drone. If you want it to go forward you have to lean it foward, if you lean it too far forward than, whoops! You crash into the ground. This makes FPV drones extremely hard to fly. Thats why I created this website! We are an all inclusive guide on everything you need to get started with this complex but extremely satisfying and fun hobby!
