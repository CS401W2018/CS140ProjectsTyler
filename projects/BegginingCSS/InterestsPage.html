What is game design?

So as the name suggests game design is the creation of games! Game design is one of my favorite hobbies to do in my spare time but it sometimes isn't easy! To make games I use the Unity Game engine, which comes with many useful tools to the creation of games. Unity is one of the most used game engines in the world used by many many games, along with Unreal engine and newcomer Godot, which has just started gaining popularity in the game development engine scene. With the Unity game engine I use the language C# to write my scripts for different gameobjects within my games. Most of the time each object you see within a game has its own script that controls how that obejct interacts with other objects within the game to make something fun to play!

Why do I love game design?

Theres a few reasons of why I love game design. The first reason is it is it's a lot of fun to create something that others can play and have fun with. The feeling of finishing a project and watching someone test my game is unparalleled, its just a really unique feeling watching someone else play something you have created. I also really enjoy it because it's a hobby that I can collaborate with other people in. Something like my other hobbies such as drone flying, there isn't nearly as much possible colaboration that you can have with others, but with game design I'm able to get to meet new people and its something I can also do together with my brother who I have a very close relationship with.
