Interest 1: Flying FPV Drones

One of my favorite things to do is fly FPV drones. These drones are special because instead of flying through a normal controller and screen, I'm wearing goggles and get to see a first person view of what the drone sees. With these drones, you're able to do a lot more than a normal drone, like performing flips and spins. The hard part about these drones is they are completely manual, so there's no fancy sensors or anything stopping the drone if you're about to hit something, so crashing is very common!
