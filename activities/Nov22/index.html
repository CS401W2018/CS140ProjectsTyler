3) How can you stay safe online?
Talked about social engineering, and just generally how they target older group of people. To protect ourself you can make sure that you don't put everything online and you keep your profiles private. You just keep in mind when you put information online hackers can use that information to preform things such as spear phishing attacks and such. The main thing though is passwords, and making sure that you use different passwords for different sites, because if one site is compramised and they didn't have proper security like using hashing and such, your credientials can be leaked and put onto things such as rainbow tables and such and it can make it really easy to be hacked. Brother slade recomends 3 passwords, one for personal credentials such as banks and such, one for emails, and one for other things. The best way he recomends is to use a password manager (gah everyone keeps telling me to get one i might finally get one smh). A password manager allows you to only have to remember one password to get in and itll generate random passwords for all your credentials for all your websites. Brother slade also recomends using things such as multifactor and 2 factor authentication when possible for things.
