Absolute vs Relative

Absolute Links

These links are the entire link. It includes the "https://", the "www." and such. Its the kind of link you put when trying to go to a website on the web! Good for linking to external websites!

Relative Links

These are good when linking to things within your own page! It is to your website only, it directs you to other files in your project!

Rules

  • Rule 1: . = means the current folder (not used on the web)
  • Rule 2: .. = means the parent folder.
  • Rule 3: / at the beginning = start at the root

Links do not only have to be inside of the nav tag! They can be placed pretty much anywhere in your website!
